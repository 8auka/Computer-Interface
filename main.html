About us


Since 2007, AC catering has been serving up the freshest baked goods and modern comfort food in a cafeteria setting. The menu is prepared simply with the freshest and best ingredients available. Afternoon Tea is served daily and includes your choice of tea and dessert and includes a fresh baked English Currant Scone. There is an extensive menu of gourmet that pair perfectly with our interesting yet approachable. Since day one, AC has been a leader is sustainable dining, original recipes, and using only the freshest ingredients. We compost our food and paper waste and recycle everything we can.

Proper Food


Our team is our best asset.

We cook from scratch each morning before dawn — handcrafting fresh, honest fare from locally sourced, organic ingredients. We cure our own heritage bacon and slow roast our own pasture-raised beef. Sustainable seafood comes to us straight from the Pacific and artisanal veggies, fruits and dairy from nearby family farms. We follow the seasons, too, and would no sooner serve tomatoes in January than parsnips in July. Every grower, rancher and supplier we partner with shares our belief that food with integrity starts from the ground up. Ocean up, too. At Proper Food, we make it a cinch to eat well on the run. We craft our fare by hand fresh every morning the only way we know how – with whole, local, organic ingredients sliced, chopped and seasoned with care. Nothing phony or factory-made. Just good, proper food that’s packed and ready to go.
